About

Sorren Jao is a web developer who is constantly learning new things. He loves learning how to code functions used in practical applications. After his years of experience with web and mobile development he has gained a basic understanding of everyday functionalities such as the built in apps on your phone, user registration form, saved databases, etc.

In his free time, Sorren enjoys watching Netflix, playing Nintendo games, or shopping on Amazon. He has a strong interest in learning how these major tech corporations build their applications for public use. Sorren hopes that he can contribute his experience towards assisting consumers. For instance, building helpful functionalities in applications, creating a user interface that improves user navigation, or suggesting new ideas that can improve user experience.

Education

School of Interactive Arts & Technology, Interactive Systems, Bachelor's of Science, Simon Fraser University

Phones are almost always carried and used by people whenever they go to work, school, or even staying at home.
